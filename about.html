Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer pet grooming services?

Yes! We have a full-service grooming salon on-site. Our professional groomers provide baths, haircuts, nail trims, and specialty styling for dogs and cats.

Can I special order products you don't have in stock?

Absolutely! If we don't have a specific product in stock, we can usually order it for you within 2-3 business days with no additional shipping fees.

Do you offer pet adoption services?

While we don't operate an adoption center ourselves, we partner with local shelters and often host adoption events on weekends. Check our social media for upcoming events.
